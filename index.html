Play against the computer in this game of chance.

How to play the game

You have five options : Rock , Paper , Scissors , Lizard , Spock .

Scissors cuts Paper Paper cover rock.

Rock crushes Lizard Lizard poisons Spock

Spock smashes Scissors Scissors decapitates Lizard

Lizard eats paper Paper disproves Spock

Spock vaporises Rock Rock crushes Scissors

You have 10 moves to score 10 points but there is a twist!

Win = 1 point to player
Lose = 1 point to the game
Draw = -1 to both

Player : 0
Game : 0

Select a button to start
